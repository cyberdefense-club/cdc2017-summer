Exercism.io is the site I told you about that gives you bite-sized Python exercises (as well as other languages).
If you are satisfied with your work on all of the other homework so far, if you are frustrated and want to do some
simpler exercises for a while, or if you just want some problems to solve to help you get better at problem-solving
in Python, set up exercism to work on your computer and see if you can finish give if them.
